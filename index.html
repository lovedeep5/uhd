About University of Houston–Downtown

UHD is a dynamic university offering bachelor’s and master’s degrees at multiple locations across Houston and online. You can design your future from an academic program that sparks your passion and connects you with fellow students, businesses and the community.

When you step onto our campus it’s like walking into the heart of downtown Houston. You’re just steps away from Fortune 500 companies, world-renowned arts organizations, award-winning restaurants, exciting entertainment and professional sports. UHD is woven into the vibrant streets of the fourth largest city in the United States and has urban appeal that celebrates the multicultural vibe of Houston.

If you’re a recent high school graduate, transfer student, or career-changing professional looking for an environment that offers flexibility and sets you up for success, UHD has the right opportunity for you.

Why Choose UHD?
Affordability

With the lowest tuition of any university in Houston, UHD provides an excellent education at an affordable rate, freeing up your income while in school, and leaving you with less debt after graduation. Our goal is to make the financial part of education as stress-free as possible so you focus on earning your degrees.

Flexibility

Work-life balance can be tough. That’s why we offer a wide range of day, evening, weekend, online and hybrid course options to fit every schedule. UHD ranks #22 among top colleges for adults returning to school according to Best College Reviews. With locations across Houston and online, we can fit any schedule!

Earning Ability

UHD ranks as one of the best Texas universities based on alumni salary. First-year earnings of UHD grads are the third highest in the state according to College Measures. A degree from UHD will set you up with a strong earning potential.
